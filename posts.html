2026-07-05: I published a conlang for the first time, and made this

I published my first conlang. That's nice. I also feel that marks a change in how I'm handling my projects; it's the first project I've published long before it is done. The rest of my projects, I have waited until I am completely done with them, and then published them.
I feel this approach is better. This way, I:

  • Probably won't have a million projects that are wip and private
  • Be able to get my ideas out there now, and potentially get feedback instead of working on them in private for like, seven months
So, i believe i'll be doing projects that way now. Yay! :D
